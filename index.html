About the Artist

Hello Everyone! I'm Samriti Dhingra from Gurugram, born on 24th December, 1998. I'm a self made artist and I'm very passionate about art. I'm a Post Graduate and completed my schooling from Sidheshwar School, Sec-9A. I love to draw, paint and do crazy stuff with colors. I recycle every possible waste around me. I teach sketching and painting with oil pastels, water colors, and soft pastel colors. If interested you can contact me directly or subscribe to my youTube channel.
